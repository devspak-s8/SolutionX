## 📅 How to Book a Project

Booking a project with SolutionX is a streamlined, technical process designed for maximum efficiency.

1.  **Select a Protocol**: Navigate to the **Pricing** section of our platform.
2.  **Choose Your Plan**: Review our specialized tiers:
    *   **Automation Starter**: For small-scale workflow optimizations.
    *   **Enterprise Solution**: Full-scale custom software and AI integration.
    *   **Technical Mentorship**: Direct guidance for internal engineering teams.
3.  **Initiate Protocol**: Click the "Initiate Protocol" button on your chosen plan. This triggers our secure intake sequence.
4.  **Technical Consultation**: Alternatively, use the **Contact** section to send a direct brief. Our lead architects typically respond within 4-6 hours with a preliminary technical assessment and scheduling link.
